1. SALARY STRUCTURE

The standard annual salary range for Software Engineers is:
- Entry Level: $80,000 - $100,000
- Mid Level: $120,000 - $150,000
- Senior Level: $160,000 - $200,000

Salaries are reviewed annually in March and adjustments are made based on 
performance evaluations. The maximum salary adjustment per year is 15%.

2. BONUS STRUCTURE

All employees are eligible for an annual performance bonus of 10-20% of 
base salary, paid in December. Bonuses are determined by:
- Individual performance rating (40% weight)
- Team performance (30% weight)
- Company profitability (30% weight)

3. BENEFITS PACKAGE

Health Insurance: Company covers 80% of premium costs
Retirement: 401(k) with 5% employer match
Vacation: 20 days per year for all employees
Sick Leave: 10 days per year

4. REMOTE WORK POLICY

Remote work is allowed up to 3 days per week. Employees must come to office
at least 2 days per week for collaboration. Manager approval required.



1. SALARY STRUCTURE

The standard annual salary range for Software Engineers is:
- Entry Level: $90,000 - $110,000
- Mid Level: $130,000 - $160,000
- Senior Level: $180,000 - $220,000

Salaries are reviewed quarterly in January, April, July, and October. 
Adjustments are made based on market rates. The maximum salary adjustment 
per year is 8%.

2. BONUS STRUCTURE

All employees are eligible for an annual performance bonus of 15-25% of 
base salary, paid in February. Bonuses are determined by:
- Individual performance rating (50% weight)
- Team performance (25% weight)
- Company profitability (25% weight)

3. BENEFITS PACKAGE

Health Insurance: Company covers 90% of premium costs
Retirement: 401(k) with 8% employer match
Vacation: 15 days per year for all employees (reduced from 20)
Sick Leave: 12 days per year

4. REMOTE WORK POLICY

All employees are fully remote. No office attendance required.
Remote work policy applies 5 days per week indefinitely.
